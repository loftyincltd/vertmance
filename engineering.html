About Vertmance Engineering & Construction

Vertmance Engineering and Construction Limited started operation in 2017, as a player in the engineering and construction industry. We had since grown from being a turnkey civil constructor & engineering firm to emerging as an apex reginal infasturctural developers.

Today, VEC boasts of over 40 years of combined working experience amongst its directors and lead Engineers, HSE Engineers etc.- and currently direct investments in infrastructure, mining, petroleum and power industries of Nigeria today. Built on strong financial and technical capacity, its mission is to develop and deliver investible bankable projects for partners.

Our execution is best in class through local teams and organizations, operating world-class standards with our global partners and capital
