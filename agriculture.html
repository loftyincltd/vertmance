About Vertmance Agric Partners

Vertmance Agric Partners (VAP) is an agro-based company focused on integrated crop and livestock production across the West-African region.
Beyond operationalizing a 42-hectares flagship OWONIKOKO Ranch in Roguwa (Nasarawa State) and our intensive grain cultivation program on over 230-hectares in Doma (Nasarawa State), our operations have extended to setting up associated out-grower farms nationwide leveraging technology. offtake and distribution of meat and crop produce accross Nigeria.

What We Do

Products & Services

Livestock Ranching


Designed to function as a combinatorial pilot, our livestock ranching practice reduces animal movements thereby fattening the livestock quicker. Our livestock are highly marketable as they yield more succulent & tender meat.

Grains & Vegetables


We are also into grains and vegetables farming due to their huge importance and versatility as human food, animal feed and valuable industrial raw material. Our crops are produced and cultivated using best agricultural practices.

Meat Processing & Retail


Our state-of-the-art meat processing facilities and MeatyMart® retail outlests provide the best cuts of meat delivered to our customer's doors at the best prices. From traditional dish cuts, to carcasses and even premium & special cuts.


Aquaculture


We have also forayed into the aquaculture business- As today aquaproduce are by far the most popular animal produce in Nigeria. Today, modern aquafarming practices like ours has made it possible for us to rear fish effectively.

Outgrower Programs


We have also extended our agricultural operations to setting-up associated out-grower farms nationwide- leveraging technology, mechanization and distribution of agri-produce through our Evergreen Cooperative.

Farm Inputs Supply


In order to fill critical agricultural service gaps, VAP has developed a market-oriented, private sector model and tools to support improved delivery of farm input supply, machinery services, training services, and technologies.

Why We Do What We Do

Our VAP Objectives

Our Company Objective
The Nigerian agro-sector is geometrically contributing to the nation's GDP- especially in light of the continuous shrink in the contribution of the oil sector. Our objective at VAP is to build support for an integrated, efficient and robust agricultural market capable of providing high-value added products for domestic and foreign markets, by leveraging on smallholder farmer networks, technology and scientific methods that boost productivity and last mile retail & distribution strategies that empowers women and youths.
Our Mission
Our mission, from day one, has always been to provide our customers with high-quality and affordable products, while building a sustainable agribusiness pegged to support both the regional and continental agricultural industry.

Our Corporate Mission

Built to support integrated, efficient and robust agro-market.
